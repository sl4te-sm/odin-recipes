Pan-fried Bacon

Description

Bacon is best when fried on the stove in its own grease.

Follow the instructions below to make pan fried bacon

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Frying pan
  • Stove
  • Optional: cooking spray

Steps

  1. Place the pan on the stove.
  2. Spray the cooking spray on the pan.
  3. Spread the bacon on the pan evenly.
  4. Set the stove to low-medium heat.
  5. Watch the bacon and flip when it starts sizzling.
  6. Continue flipping the bacon every minute or two.
  7. Remove the bacon once it is all brick red.
