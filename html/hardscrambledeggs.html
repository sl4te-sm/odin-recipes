Hard Scrambled Eggs

Description

Eggs are subjective; some like them boiled soft, but I like them scrambled hard.

Here are some instructions on scrambling them hard (AKA burnt)

This dude likes his eggs burnt brown!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Skillet
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Whisk
  • Bowl
  • Stove

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Mix milk into the eggs.
  3. Beat the eggs with the whisk until they're yellow.
  4. Heat up skillet on stove to low-medium heat.
  5. Melt butter on the skillet.
  6. Pour eggs into the skillet.
  7. Watch and stir eggs every 30 seconds.
  8. Flip eggs once they are sticking to the skillet.
  9. Continue flipping eggs until they are light brown.
  10. Remove eggs from the skillet.
