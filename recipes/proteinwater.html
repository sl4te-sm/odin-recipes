Protein Water

Introduction

Protein shakes with milk taste better, but water is easier to drink.

Here's how to make protein water.

Note: this is protein water - not protein shake.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Protein Powder
  • Blender
  • Glass

Steps

  1. Pour water into the blender.
  2. Pour protein into the blender.
  3. Blend the protein and water for 15 seconds.
  4. Pour the protein water into a glass.
